Learn or Improve Your Tlingit

Our Tlingit identity is born through our language, inherited through our mothers. In our traditional way of life, we know one another. We know the lineage of one another and what that means to us and to our clans and families. This is a difficult world. In Lingít we might say «yatʼéexʼi át áyá yá k̲usti» (this life is hard, frozen). As a people, we are faced with linguistic extinction. As a world, we are faced with choices of what to let go of, often by doing nothing about it.
